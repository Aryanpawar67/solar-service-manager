Build a complete **Solar Panel Service Management System (GreenVolt)** consisting of:

## 🎯 SYSTEM OVERVIEW

A full-stack platform that manages:

* Customers
* Staff
* Service scheduling
* Subscription plans
* Payments
* Notifications
* Field service execution via Android app

The system should include:

1. Admin Web Dashboard
2. Customer Website
3. Staff Android App
4. (Optional) Customer Mobile App

---

# 🌐 1. ADMIN PANEL (WEB DASHBOARD)

## Core Features:

### 👤 Customer Management

* Add / Edit / Delete customers
* Store:
there should be one form to be filled by customers 
  * Name
  * Phone number
  * Address (Google Maps integration)
  * Solar panel capacity
  * Installation details

---

### 👨‍🔧 Staff Management

* Add / Manage staff
* Assign:

  * Work area
  * Availability
  * Contact details

---

### 📅 Service Management

* Create service jobs
* Assign staff
* Select service date
* Add notes/instructions
* Track status:

  * Pending
  * In Progress
  * Completed

---

### 🔔 Notifications & Reminders

* Auto reminders before service date
* Notifications via:

  * Push notifications
  * SMS
  * WhatsApp

---

### 📊 Dashboard Analytics

* Total customers
* Active subscriptions
* Completed services
* Pending services
* Revenue tracking

---

### 📥 Contact Form Integration

* Website "Contact Us" submissions stored automatically in admin panel

---

### 📅 Schedule Visibility

* View:

  * All staff schedules
  * All customer service schedules

---

# 📱 2. STAFF ANDROID APP

## Features:

### 📅 Schedule Dashboard

* View assigned services:

  * Today
  * Upcoming
  * Completed

---

### 📍 Customer Details

* Name
* Phone number
* Address (open in Google Maps navigation)

---

### 🧰 Service Workflow

1. Start Service
2. Capture **Pre-Service Photo**
3. Perform cleaning/service
4. Capture **Post-Service Photo**
5. Submit service report

---

### 📤 Report Generation

* Auto-generate PDF report including:

  * Before/After images
  * Service date/time
  * Staff name
  * Remarks

---

### 📲 WhatsApp Integration

* Send report automatically to customer via WhatsApp

---

# 💰 3. SUBSCRIPTION SYSTEM

## Plan Types:

* Basic (1 visit/month)
* Standard (2 visits/month)
* Premium (4 visits/month)

---

## Features:

* Subscribe to plans
* Auto-generate service schedules monthly
* Auto-renew subscription
* Pause / Cancel subscription
* Reminder before expiry

---

# 💳 4. PAYMENT INTEGRATION

Integrate with Razorpay for:

* UPI payments
* Cards
* Net banking

---

## Payment Flow:

1. Customer selects plan
2. Creates order via backend
3. Opens payment gateway
4. Verifies payment
5. Activates subscription
